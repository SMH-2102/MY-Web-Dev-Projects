A peek at his life

  1. Nikola Tesla was born on 10-July 1856 in Austrian Empire
  2. 1870s-He studied engineering and physics without recieving a degree.
  3. 1880s-He started acquiring practical experience through working in telephony and Continental Edison.
  4. 1884-Immigrated to the United States
  5. He worked for a short while at Edison Machine Works then started his own enterprise.
  6. Tesla set up laboratories and companies in New York to develop a range of electrical and mechanical devices.
  7. 1888-His works like AC induction motor and related polyphase AC patents were licensed by Westinghouse Electric.
  8. Tesla conducted a range of experiments with mechanical oscillators/generators, electrical discharge tubes, and early X-ray imaging. He also built a wirelessly controlled boat
  9. Tesla pursued his ideas for wireless lighting and worldwide wireless electric power distribution in his high-voltage, high-frequency power experiments in New York and Colorado Springs
  10. Tesla tried to put these ideas to practical use in his unfinished Wardenclyffe Tower project, an intercontinental wireless communication and power transmitter, but ran out of funding before he could complete it.
  11. He is widely known for his invention of tesla coil in 1889.
  12. After the success of his Tesla coil there was a chance of revolution in the power industry.
  13. Tesla did deep research on the ionosphere(a layer of ions present around the earth) layer.
  14. He wanted to provide free electricity to each household in America.
  15. Although his ideas could revolutionize the world the indrustialist were unhappy with such a proposal.
  16. This lead to widthdrawal of funds from the indrustialists.
  17. In 1960s the General Conference on Weights and Measures acknowledged his work and named the unit of magnetic flux density tesla in his honor.
